# Supplement Picture Steering

## Goal

Supplement pictures should look like clean product photography while avoiding medical or prescription-like claims.

## Required Style

- Show capsules, powder, bottle, jar, scoop, or clean supplement product form.
- Use a white, transparent, or very bright neutral background.
- Avoid medical imagery, hospitals, needles, pills scattered messily, or alarming visuals.
- Avoid brand logos unless there is permission to use them.
- Keep labels generic unless the product is intentionally branded.

## Validation Rule

Before accepting a supplement image, check:

1. Is the supplement form obvious?
2. Is the background clean and bright?
3. Does it avoid medical/prescription framing?
4. Does it avoid unsupported health claims in the image itself?
