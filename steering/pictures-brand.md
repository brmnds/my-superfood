## My Superfood Brand

- Keep the My Superfood brand bright, organic, and trustworthy.
- Use natural green as the main brand signal.
- Avoid dark themes, purple gradients, decorative orbs, and artificial wellness stock imagery.

## Validation Rule

Before accepting a brand/integration image, check:

1. Is it the correct brand asset?
2. Is it locally stored and not hotlinked?
3. Does it render clearly at small and large sizes?
4. Does it avoid conflicting with the My Superfood visual direction?
