# Recipe Picture Steering

## Goal

Recipe pictures should feel editorial, healthy, bright, and slightly boho. Unlike food ingredient pictures, recipe pictures may show plated dishes and styled scenes.

## Required Style

- Show the finished dish clearly.
- Use bright natural light.
- Prefer organic textures: ceramic, linen, wood, herbs, fresh ingredients.
- Keep the dish appetizing and clean.
- Avoid dark, moody, heavily filtered, or stock-like images.
- Avoid clutter that makes the dish hard to inspect.
- Use a small gallery only when each image adds useful context.

## Composition

- Main recipe image: full dish or bowl, composed cleanly.
- Gallery strip: ingredient detail, alternate angle, or close-up.
- Keep all gallery images visually compatible with the main image.

## Validation Rule

Before accepting a recipe image, check:

1. Does the dish look healthy and fresh?
2. Is the food the clear subject?
3. Does the image match the bright organic My Superfood visual direction?
4. Is it distinct from food ingredient cards, which should remain raw-product style?
